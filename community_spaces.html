COMMUNITY SPACES

Gathering, Growing, and Thriving

Douglass Park & Recreation

Spanning over 173 acres, Douglass Park is the green heart of the West Side. It features lagoons, gymnasiums, and sports fields that host summer festivals and daily community life.

Urban Gardens & Greening

Local residents have transformed vacant lots into vibrant community gardens. These spaces not only provide fresh produce but also serve as peaceful sanctuaries and outdoor classrooms for youth.

The Firehouse Community Arts Center

A historic firehouse turned into a safe haven for young people. Through arts, mentorship, and culinary training, this center empowers the next generation of North Lawndale leaders.
